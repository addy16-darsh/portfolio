Values & Goals

I'm driven by innovation with purpose, guided by the philosophy:

"Karmanye vadhikaraste, ma phaleshu kadachana" — You have the right to work, but not to the fruits of action.

This principle inspires me to work sincerely, focus on meaningful effort, and contribute without attachment to outcomes. I strive to support and uplift those around me, valuing friendship, empathy, and collaboration as central pillars of both personal and professional life. My long-term goal is to create technology that empowers people, strengthens trust in AI systems, and reflects integrity, compassion, and social responsibility.
